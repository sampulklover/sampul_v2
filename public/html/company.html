Our mission

To empower individuals to preserve their digital legacies, ensuring a seamless and secure transfer of their online presence to future generations.
Empowerment
We believe in empowering individuals to take control of their digital legacies. We provide the tools and knowledge needed to secure their online presence for the benefit of loved ones.
Security
The security of our users' data is our top priority. We employ cutting-edge encryption and stringent security measures to ensure that sensitive information remains confidential and protected.
Simplicity
We strive to make digital estate planning as simple and accessible as possible. Our user-friendly platform removes complexities, making it easy for anyone to create a digital will.
Collaboration
Collaboration is at the heart of what we do. We enable users to work closely with trusted individuals (Co-SAMPUL) to ensure their digital wishes are carried out effectively.
Innovation
We are committed to innovation in digital estate planning. We continuously seek new ways to improve our platform and offer users the best possible experience.  
Legacy
Preserving legacies is our core mission. We honor the memories, experiences, and digital assets that individuals want to pass on to future generations, helping to create lasting connections and understanding across time.

Details

SAMPUL makes digital estate planning a team effort. You can appoint a Co-SAMPUL, a trusted person like a family member, friend, or advisor, to assist with your digital will. In case of your passing, their role is to inform SAMPUL, which will then unlock and deliver your digital will. Your wishes remain private and secure throughout.
SAMPUL also allows secure sharing of your digital will and asset information with chosen individuals, ensuring a smooth transition of your digital assets when the time comes. This collaborative approach simplifies digital legacy management.
